Questions? We've Got Answers

How is this different from just researching online?
You could absolutely do this research yourself—but it would take 40-60 hours per location, and you'd need to know which sources to trust. We've spent 500+ hours building our methodology and relationships with data sources. You're paying for speed, accuracy, and expertise.
What if I don't like the locations you recommend?
We don't "recommend" locations in the abstract—we analyze them. Our reports give you data on both strengths AND weaknesses so you can make an informed decision. Some clients use reports to rule OUT locations, which is just as valuable.
How current is your data?
We update our database continuously. Newsletter analysis uses the most current data available at publication. Custom reports are current as of the report date, which we clearly note.
Do you have affiliate relationships with real estate agents?
No. We have no financial relationships with agents, developers, or local economic offices. Our only revenue comes from subscriptions and custom reports. This ensures our analysis remains unbiased.
